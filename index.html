Case Snapshot

People-First

Hire for attitude, train for skill; empowerment & transparency

Open-Book

Financial literacy & ownership mindset ("Zodiak" game)

Safety & Service

Industry-leading safety record & high customer satisfaction

Leadership continuity (Kelleher → Kelly), University for People, strong union partnerships, and a fun, high-trust culture underpin performance.

Foundations: Leadership & EI

Styles in Play

  • Transformational — purpose, values, inspiration
  • Servant — empower frontlines; remove blockers
  • Situational — adapt across operations vs. customer care

Emotional Intelligence

  • Empathy & trust → psychological safety
  • Self/other awareness → better conflict navigation
  • Social skill → cross-functional collaboration

High-Performance Teams

  • Psychological safety → voice, creativity, resilience
  • Clear goals & shared mental models (ops ↔ service)
  • Cadence: brief → execute → debrief (Everest-style loops)
  • Blake–Mouton: high concern for people & results

Behaviors: speak up, help first, customer-back decisions, data-visible to all.

Diagnosis: What Could Derail Performance?

  • Profit pressure vs. culture (fatigue; morale risk)
  • Union negotiations → trust frictions
  • Social media incidents → decision bias under stress
  • Scaling learning in hybrid/AI-augmented work

Risks & Mitigations

  • Comms fatigue → concise, visual, frontline-led updates
  • Tool sprawl → standardize stacks; enablement sprints
  • Value drift → values in OKRs; leadership 360s; story sharing

Takeaways

  • People-first isn’t a slogan — it’s an operating system
  • Psychological safety drives high performance
  • Ethical, bias-aware decisions protect brand & trust
  • Learning culture + empowerment → durable advantage

Lead with heart; scale with systems.
